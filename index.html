The Story of Dark Knight Arena

In a world shrouded in eternal night, where the sun has not risen for centuries, the realm's greatest warriors gather in a sacred arena. This is the Dark Knight Arena, a place where legends are forged in the heat of battle. Once a peaceful kingdom, it was plunged into darkness by a vengeful sorcerer. Now, only the bravest and most skilled knights can hope to lift the curse and restore light to the land. As a contender in the arena, you must fight not just for glory, but for the fate of the world. Will you rise to the challenge and become the champion who brings dawn back to the realm?
